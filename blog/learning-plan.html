Learning Plan

My long term goal is to have the career and lifestyle flexibility that comes with being a skilled software developer.

I have a lot of patience and focus which allows me to dig into the nitty gritty even when it isn't a very pleasurable experience. I never doubt my technical abilities. Even if I feel completely lost I always know it is simply a matter of time before I see how all the pieces fit. On the other hand, when the challenge is non-technical my confidence can waver and I can begin to lose focus. I would like to see my ability to collaborate and present ideas to others improve, and I would like to gain further confidence in negotiating with others. I also spent so long touching grass with the working class at New World that I need to reacclimate to a corporate environment.

I plan to maintain resilience by taking quality breaks from study, doing things I value or enjoy. Running, hiking and weightlifting, lunch or dinner breaks with friends and family, progressing important personal projects where I can. Many times when I have felt overwhelmed or I have begun to lose sight of the greater purpose of my efforts, going for a hike has allowed me to regain perspective, see the challenges ahead of me clearly and restore my resilience. I have a couple of close friends I am frequently in contact with that I can reach out to for support if things get tough. Family are also close by but eww family.

I expect facilitators to be available to respond to queries about the course and expectations, particularly during bootcamp. I expect a degree of professionalism, empathy and charitability from the facilitation team.

I do not anticipate any commitments getting in the way of my studies.
